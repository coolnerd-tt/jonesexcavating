## Anticipated questions + answers

**Q: What if Anthropic raises prices or shuts down?**
The system is model-agnostic. We can swap to OpenAI, Mistral, or a self-hosted model in a few hours. Prompts are portable.

**Q: What if our office manager doesn't want to do this?**
The 15-minute-a-day routine is a single inbox with edit/approve buttons. If she'd rather not, we stay on Concierge ($1,900/mo) longer. No pressure to step down tiers on a fixed timeline.

**Q: What about social platforms changing their APIs?**
That's part of what the maintenance retainer covers — quarterly review, integration patches as Google/Meta/LinkedIn shift. Built into the $350/mo Self-serve tier.

**Q: Can we pilot just one piece first?**
Yes. We can launch the Field-to-Content + Reputation agents in week 4 of the build, before the full website is live. Gives them a 60-day runway to evaluate before fully committing.

**Q: Who owns the data?**
You do. All approved content, brand voice config, and historical drafts live in your tenant config. Walk-out clause: if you ever leave, you take the whole system.

**Q: What about Callus — is the brand decision locked?**
Spark direction is the recommended path and what the v4 proposal reflects. Kintsugi is dropped per last conversation. Final call still yours before brand work starts in week 1.
